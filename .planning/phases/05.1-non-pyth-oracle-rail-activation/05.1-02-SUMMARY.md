## The cluster-redeploy correction
The first broadcast (CR `0x203e…`, SM `0x019d…`) redeployed only CR+SM. Because `FollowFadeMarket.callRegistry` and `ChallengeEscrow.callRegistry`/`.followFadeMarket` are `immutable`, that left FFM/CE bound to the old CR and `new CR.followFadeMarket=0` (so `createCall.initPool` would silently skip). The script was corrected to redeploy CR+FFM+CE+SM as a consistent cluster with cluster-consistency assertions; `0x203e/0x019d` are abandoned.

## On-chain verification (all PASS)
`adapterMap(2,1)=2`, `(2,6)=4` (Snapshot), `(2,7)=5` (Tally), `(0,0)=0` (Pyth unaffected); `attestationSigner(1-6)` all = the derived KMS addresses; every internal ref points inside the cluster.
