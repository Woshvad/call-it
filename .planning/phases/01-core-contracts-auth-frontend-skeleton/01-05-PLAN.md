Vertical slice A: a real user lands on `/signin`, signs in via one of three Privy paths (Connect Wallet, Google, Twitter), and arrives at `/` with an authenticated wagmi session — including OAuth paths having auto-created an embedded Privy wallet and Twitter pre-linking its handle. Purpose: This is the first user-visible Phase 1 capability. Critical-path step 1 (Sign-up) lands here. Plans 06/08/09 depend on the provider tree being correct and the session being live. Pitfall 13 (provider order regression silently kills OAuth wallets) is the highest-impact risk in this slice — the AST test is what makes the invariant durable across future refactors.

Output: A functioning sign-in flow with the AST test, e2e test, and config files in place; ready for Plan 06 to layer the 4-screen onboarding on top.
</objective>
